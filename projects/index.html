Ongoing

Analytic marginalization

We are developing a new approximate method of analytic marginalization which correctly takes into account correlation between cosmological and nuisance parameters.

Modifying primordial power spectrum for the Hubble Tension

Based on Phys.Rev.Lett. 130, 161003 (2023), we try to find a modification of primordial power spectrum as a solution to the Hubble tension.

Modifying the Hubble rate in early-time for the Hubble Tension

Based on Phys.Rev.Lett. 130, 161003 (2023), we try to find a modification of the Hubble rate in early Universe as a solution to the Hubble tension.

Improvement on Galaxy Redshift-Space Distortion (RSD) Model

Studying possible improvement on the current RSD model

DM-Baryon Scattering with Fokker-Planck Operator
